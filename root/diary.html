Dear Diary

Some days, everything aligns into greatness. And on some days, you just can't win! Here are two recent examples.

Friday April 7, 2017

The Best Day Ever!

I was contacted by a spirit who said their name was "Father Yod". It was an amazing morning I won't forget.

First, I want to say this happened. I'm not making it up. I was drinking some tea in my kitchen and was taking a sip, when this piercing green light flooded my kitchen. I dropped my cup and I don't remember hearing it break. This wooly silence was in the room and it was bright green. Then a face materialized out of the color and it was a smiling older man. He was laughing and I didn't fear him at all. His lips were moving, but I couldn't hear him. I mumbled "I can't hear you." And he cleared his throat and then it came though clearly, "sorry, I haven't visited anyone in a long time. My name is Father Yod. I am here to, if you want, take away your fear. Do you want me to?"

Friday April 28, 2017

The Worst Day Ever!

I ran out of half-and-half. I mean, NO half-and-half.

Following the crazy event of a couple weeks back, after which I didn't go to work and laid about in a stupor, I thought I had experienced weird stuff. But then I learned that my roommate Kevin drank the last of the half-and-half and didn't replace it! He knows how much I need it for my coffee. We had that talk several times. Now I can't even think about Father Yod. And I stubbed my toe really bad on Kevin's stupid sculpture. Which made the day even worse.
